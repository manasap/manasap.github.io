Who we are

Decor with a personal, celebratory touch.

Next Door Decors is a Northern Virginia-based decor team focused on helping clients celebrate life’s special moments with custom setups, gifts, and event styling.

The brand language across the brochures blends earthy greens, creams, browns, and soft neutrals, so this website direction uses that same calm, elevated palette for a simple static launch.

Services

What we offer

Custom Event Decor

Styled setups for pooja events, mehendi, haldi, sangeeth, birthdays, bridal showers, baby showers, proposals, graduations, and more.

Rentals & Grab-and-Go

Rentals include carts, favor walls, and grab-and-go decor packages for smaller celebrations and flexible budgets.

Gift Packages & Add-Ons

Gift displays, flower boxes, flower frames, custom signs, floral arrangements, and cutouts to complete the event story.

Our process

Simple booking flow

1

Initial phone call

Discuss event requirements, vision, and details.

2

Rough quote & proposal

Receive inspiration ideas, decor direction, and a preliminary quote.

3

Revisions

Refine the concept until the final design and pricing are aligned.

4

Reservation & deposit

Secure the booking with a 50% deposit through the online portal, card, or Zelle.

5

Event day setup

The team handles setup and teardown on the event day.
