Who we are

A decor studio for meaningful celebrations.

Next Door Decors is a Northern Virginia-based decor team creating custom setups, grab-and-go decor, gift packages, rentals, and event styling for birthdays, baby showers, bridal showers, proposals, poojas, mehendis, haldi events, corporate gatherings, and more.

We design decor that goes beyond templates—bringing together Indian traditions and modern Western aesthetics to create one-of-a-kind setups that feel cohesive, elegant, and memorable.
